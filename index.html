What it is:

Tripfag-Finder is an easy-to-use userscript for finding threads on /b/.
It works with Greasemonkey-compatible software, including Firefox, Google Chrome, and Opera.

How to get it:

Simply download and install Tripfag-Finder.user.js by clicking on "Download Userscript" box.
On Chrome, this may require the additional step of going to chrome://extensions and dragging the file onto that page.
